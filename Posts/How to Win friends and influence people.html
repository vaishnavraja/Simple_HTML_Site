How to win friends and Influence people

Nine suggestions on how to get most out of the book

  1. Have a deep driving desire to learn, determination to increase your ability to deal with people
  2. Re-read each chapter thoughtfully
  3. Stop frequently to ask yourself how you can apply each suggestions
  4. Mark suggestions that you feel you can use, easier to review
  5. After reading the book, spend few hours reviewing it every month
  6. Apply the rules at every opportunity. Only knowledge that is used stock to your mind
  7. Offer spouse/child/business associate a dime every time they catch you breaking the rules
  8. Check up each week on the progress you’re making. Ask yourself what mistakes you have made, what improvement
  9. Keep as record showing how and when you have applied these principles

Part 1- Fundamental techniques in handling people

If you want to gather honey, don’t kick over the beehive

  1. Criticisms are like homing pigeons, they always return home. Sharp criticisms and rebukes almost always end in futility.
  2. Instead of condemning people, try to understand them. Try to figure out why they do what they do. It breeds sympathy, tolerance and kindness. To know all is to forgive all.
Principle1- Don’t criticise, condemn of complain

The big secret of dealing with people

  1. The only way to get anybody do anything is by making the other prison want to do it.
  2. The deepest urge inhuman nature is the desire to be important- John Dewey.
  3. Ability to create enthusiasm in people is the greatest asset one can possess.
  4. The way to develop the best in a person is by appreciation and encouragement.
  5. The difference between flattery and appreciation- one is sincere and the other isn’t.
  6. Try to figure out other person’s good points. Then forget flattery. Give honest, sincere appreciation.
Principle 2- Give honest and sincere appreciation.

He who can do this has the whole world with him. He who cannot walks a lonely way.
